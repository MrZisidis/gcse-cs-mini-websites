🏗️ Infrastructure Scenarios

Small Office Floor

Star Topology + Cat6 Copper

Justification: A central switch allows high-speed 1Gbps connections. If one cable fails, the rest of the network stays online. Copper is cost-effective for distances under 100m.

Inter-Building Link

Point-to-Point + Fiber Optic

Justification: Fiber uses light pulses, making it immune to electrical interference between buildings. It supports 10Gbps+ speeds over many kilometers without signal loss.

Hospital/School Campus

Mesh Topology + Wi-Fi 6

Justification: Provides high mobility for users. Mesh topology allows nodes to route traffic around dead zones or failed units, ensuring constant uptime for critical applications.
